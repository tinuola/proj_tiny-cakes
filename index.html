About Us

At Tiny Cakes Bakery, we bake cupcakes just like Grandma used to, but just a few sizes smaller. We start from scratch using the freshest, organic, non-GMO ingredients. If you can't pronounce it, we don't bake with it!

Stop by our San Francisco locations for daily mini treats or place deliveries online. Got an event coming up and need something unique? Call our bakeries to discuss custom orders. Anything you can dream up, we can make!

History

Hello! I'm Tracey Anderson and I own Tiny Cakes Bakery. I'm a big gal (6' 0"!) who loves tiny things and cupcakes. One day, I thought, "Why not combine the two?" And the idea for Tiny Cakes Bakery was born.

I started baking miniature cupcakes for friends who referred me to their friends, who referred me to more friends... . Soon I was covered in cake mix 24-7 and outgrowing my tiny (yes...) kitchen.

The first Tiny Cakes Bakery opened in San Francisco in 2011, and we've been hard-at-play creating bite-sized sweetness for lovers of cupcakes ever since.
